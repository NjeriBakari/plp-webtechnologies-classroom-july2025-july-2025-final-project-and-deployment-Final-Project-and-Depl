About Our Cakes

Handcrafted with love, baked to perfection

Our Story

At Kipepeo Delights, we believe every celebration deserves a memorable cake. Founded in 2020, our bakery has been bringing joy to families and events across Nairobi with our delicious, handcrafted cakes.

Our skilled bakers use only the finest ingredients to create delicious, beautiful cakes that will make your special moments even sweeter. From intimate gatherings to grand celebrations, we're here to add that perfect touch of sweetness to your day.

Our Cake Varieties

Chocolate Cakes

Rich, moist chocolate cakes made with premium cocoa. Available in dark chocolate, milk chocolate, and chocolate fudge varieties.

Vanilla Cakes

Classic vanilla cakes with a light, fluffy texture. Perfect for any occasion and can be customized with various frostings.

Red Velvet Cakes

Elegant red velvet cakes with cream cheese frosting. A customer favorite for weddings and special events.

Fruit Cakes

Fresh fruit-topped cakes with light sponge and whipped cream. Available with seasonal fruits.

Custom Cakes

Personalized cakes designed to match your theme. From birthdays to weddings, we bring your vision to life.

Cupcakes

Delightful individual-sized treats in various flavors. Perfect for parties and events.
