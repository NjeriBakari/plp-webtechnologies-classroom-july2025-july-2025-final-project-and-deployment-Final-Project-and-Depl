Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I order?

We recommend ordering at least 3-5 days in advance for custom cakes. For large events, please order 1-2 weeks ahead.

Do you offer delivery?

Yes! We offer delivery within Nairobi. Delivery charges vary based on location.

Can I customize the design?

Absolutely! We specialize in custom designs. Share your vision with us and we'll bring it to life.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa, bank transfers, and cash payments. A 50% deposit is required to confirm your order.
